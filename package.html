Photography & Videography FAQ

We specialize in weddings, engagements, pre-weddings, maternity shoots, baby showers, corporate events, product shoots, fashion, drone cinematography, cinematic films, and customized packages tailored to your unique needs.

We recommend booking at least 3–6 months in advance to secure your preferred date. For peak wedding seasons, early booking (up to a year ahead) is highly suggested.

We use high-end DSLR and mirrorless cameras (Canon, Sony, Nikon), 4K/8K video cameras, drones, gimbals, sliders, and professional lighting setups to capture premium-quality visuals.

Yes, we design luxury albums (with glossy, matte, or embossed finishes) and offer mini-books, calendars, and digital copies. Every album is custom-designed to narrate your story beautifully.

Delivery timelines depend on the project size: - Wedding photos: 4–6 weeks - Wedding films: 6–8 weeks - Short shoots/events: 1–3 weeks (We also provide teaser photos/videos within a few days of your event.)
