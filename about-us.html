About Marvel Studio
Capturing timeless moments with creativity, passion, and storytelling.

Marvel Creation is an endeavor dedicated to achieving that very goal of photography. We are a Gujarat-based firm that specializes in candid wedding photography and videography and we are also a dedicated group of Indian wedding photographers and candid cinematographers who enjoy capturing and preserving the most beautiful emotions.

We operate in photographic studio with a wealth of experience. We're obsessed with upcoming unique, off-the-beaten-path wedding ideas.
